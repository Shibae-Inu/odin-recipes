Musubi

Today we are going to be making Musubi. I am from Hawaii and I grew up eating this popular and easy to make snack.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz Spam
  • 1/4 cup oysster sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • Nori(seaweed) cut into halves or thirds
  • 6 cups cooked sushi rice - without rice vinegar

Directions

  1. slice the spam into 8-10 slices and put in container with mixed oyster suace, soy sauce, and sugar and marinate for about 15-20 minutes
  2. Drain off marinade and fry spam on each side on medium until desired doneness
  3. Place strip of nori down first then place your musubi mold across the middle and add sushi rice and press down evenly.
  4. Remove the mold from the rice and add a piece of spam ontop of it and wrap the nori around the spam
  5. Use a little water to make nori adhere to each other and tightly pack the musubi
  6. Keep repeating til all the spams are made into musubi.
  7. Enjoy!
