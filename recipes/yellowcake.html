Yellow Cake

Today we are going to be making a simple Yellow cake that tastes delicious and easy to make

Ingredients

  • Buttermilk
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • semi-sweet chocolate
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Corn Starch
  • Baking Powder
  • Cocoa Powder

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350F and butter and flour 3-6in pans.
  2. Sift flour, corn starch, baking powder, and salt ito a large bowl and whisk together and set aside
  3. Cream butter and sugar in a stand mixer using a paddle attatchment.
  4. Once light and fluffy, pour in oil and vanilla then mix until combined.
  5. Add the eggs and yolks one at a time while mixing on medium-low then scrape bowl down.
  6. Add the flour mixture in three batches alternating witht he buttermilk, mix until just combined then distribute batter evenly in the 3 prepared cake pans and bake for about 30 minutes at 350F.
  7. While the cake layers are cooling, you can whip up the chocolate buttercream. First in a small bowl melt the semi-sweet chocolate with 1/4 cup of milk, 30 seconds in the microwave should be okay. In another bowl mix the cocoa powder with 1.4 cup of melted butter.
  8. Cream the butter then pour in the cocoa powder/butter mixture and mix until combined. Scrape the bowl down and mix once more.
  9. Add the salt and powdered sugar then mix until incorporated. Beat in the ganahe and then add milk a tablespoon at a time unil silky smooth
  10. pipe buttcream onto the first layer and add the next layer on and repeat until the cake is built
  11. Cover the cake with butter cream with a a spatula until smooth.
  12. grab a knife to cut slices and serve
